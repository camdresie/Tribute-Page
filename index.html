Queen of the Gays

Lady Gaga at the release of her 2016 album, Joanne.

A Timeline of Lady Gaga's life:

  • 1986: Lady Gaga is born Stefanie Joanne Angelina Germanotta in New York City
  • 2005: Lady Gaga begins her musical career and ascent to greatness
  • 2008: Lady Gaga's first album, The Fame, is released
  • 2011: Lady Gaga's second album, Born This Way, is released
  • 2013: Lady Gaga's third album, Artpop, is released
  • 2016: Lady Gaga's fourth album, Joanne, is released
  • 2020: Lady Gaga's fifth album, Chromatica, will be released
